Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna is perfect for any occasion, and features a delightful cheesy filling. Instead of the traditional ricotta, this recipe uses a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan for a unique twist. Perfectly paired with a fresh green salad and crusty garlic bread, this lasagna is a family favorite that never disappoints.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 32 ounces cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 32 ounce jar spaghetti sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • ½ cup water

Directions

  1. Gather all the ingredients and preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook and stir the ground beef until it is browned and crumbly, which should take about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain the grease, then add the spaghetti sauce and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, eggs, half of the grated Parmesan cheese, dried parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread 3/4 cup of the sauce. Layer with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cups of the cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup of sauce. Repeat the layers once more. Finish with the remaining 3 noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. Pour 1/2 cup of water along the edges of the dish and cover it tightly with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Then, uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes. Allow it to stand for 10 minutes before serving.
