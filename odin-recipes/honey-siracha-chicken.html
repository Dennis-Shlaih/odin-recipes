Honey Siracha Chicken Recipe

Description

This honey Sriracha Chicken is a deliciously spicy and sweet dish featuring tender chunks of chicken breasts coated in a flavorful sauce made from sriracha, honey, soy sauce, and garlic. It’s perfect for those who love a bit of heat balanced with sweetness. Serve it over cooked rice and sprinkle with crushed red pepper flakes for an extra kick.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 inch chunks chicken breasts
  • Corn starch
  • 2-3 tablespoons sriracha
  • Soy sauce
  • Minced garlic
  • Sugar
  • A couple tablespoons honey
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Cooked rice for serving (optional)

Directions

  1. In a small or medium saucepan, mix together 1 cup of water, sriracha, soy sauce, garlic, sugar, and honey. Stir well and bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat.
  2. While the sauce is heating, dissolve 2 tablespoons of cornstarch in 2 tablespoons of water. Add this mixture to the saucepan and stir until the sauce thickens. Then, reduce the heat to low.
  3. Place the diced chicken and 1/3 cup of cornstarch in a large plastic bag. Seal the bag and shake to coat the chicken evenly. Heat oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat, then add the coated chicken and sauté until browned and fully cooked.
  4. Pour the sauce over the chicken and stir to coat all pieces evenly.
  5. Optionally, sprinkle with crushed pepper flakes and serve over warm rice.
